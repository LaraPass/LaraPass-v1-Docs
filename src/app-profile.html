Profile

User Profile displays allot of sensitive and valuable information of the User. User's can change their Name, DOB, Mobile No. & Country.

User's can also change the Password Randomizer (RPG) Preset (1 to 4) & their Support PIN according to their requirement.

They can also change their Avatar, Account Password and View Login IP Logs.
